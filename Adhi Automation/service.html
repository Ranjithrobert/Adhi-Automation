We also manufacture copper alloy precision components, weighing from 50 grams to 25 kgs. The melting capacity of the foundry is 3000 kgs per day. We are handling the alloys of the following specifications:

  • BS 1400 Series:

    • PB 1, PB 2, PB 4, CT 1, LB 1, LB 1, LB 2, LB 4, LB 5, LG 1, LG 2 & LG 4.

  • IS 318 Series:

    • LTB 1, LTB 2, LTB 3, LTB 4, LTB 5 & LTB 6..

  • IS28 Series:

    • GR 1, GR 2, GR 3, GR 4 & GR 5.

  • DIN 1705 Series:

    • G Cu Sn 12, G Cu Sn 12 Ni, G Cu Sn 10, G Cu Sn 10 Zn, G Cu Sn 7 Zn Pb, G Cu Sn 5 Zn Pb.

The industry has an in house quality laboratory with full time chemist to monitor the chemistry of the alloys, thus ensuring the composition of the materials. The Laboratory is equipped with Electrolytic analyzer, Muffle Furnaces, High precision weighing machine and other equipment Required for quality and accurate Analysis of the composition of the alloys.
