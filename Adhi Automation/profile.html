PROFILE

Adhi Automation is an ISO 9001: 2000 certified market leader in the manufacturing and supplying of precision turned & machined components and Sub assemblies in all categories of Ferrous and Non- Ferrous materials. Strategically located in one of India’s leading industrial centres, Coimbatore.

CERTIFICATION

The commitment towards quality and the capable control system of Adhi Automation are certified for ISO 9001: 2000 standard by M/s. TUV Suddeutschland.
